#### Phthalimide to Potassium Phthalimide
```smiles
C(=O)2c1ccccc1C(=O)N2.[R]O.[K]O>>C(=O)2c1ccccc1C(=O)[N-]2[K+]
```
#### N-Alkylphthalimide
```smiles
C(=O)2c1ccccc1C(=O)[N-]2[K+].[R][X]>>C(=O)2c1ccccc1C(=O)N2[R]
```

#### 1$\degree$ Amine
```smiles
C(=O)2c1ccccc1C(=O)N2[R].O.[Na]O>>[Na]OC(=O)c1ccccc1C(=O)O[Na].N[R]
```
> **Limitation:** Cannot prepare aromatic primary amines (aryl halides don't undergo nucleophilic substitution with phthalimide anion).
