### Physical Properties
- **General Formula**: $\ce{RN2+X^{-},}X \in [Cl^{-}, Br^{-}, HSO_{4}^{-}, BF_{4}^-]$
- **N₂⁺ Group:** Diazonium group
- **Aliphatic Diazonium Salts:** Highly unstable
- **Arenediazonium Salts:** Stable for a short time in solution at low temperatures (273-278 K) due to resonance stabilization. 
### Importance of Diazonium Salts in Synthesis
- Excellent intermediates for introducing -F, -Cl, -Br, -I, -CN, -OH, -NO₂ groups into aromatic rings.
- Allows synthesis of aryl fluorides and iodides (not possible by direct halogenation).
- Cyanobenzene easily obtained from diazonium salt (not by nucleophilic substitution of chlorobenzene).
- Helpful in preparing substituted aromatic compounds not accessible by direct substitution.
