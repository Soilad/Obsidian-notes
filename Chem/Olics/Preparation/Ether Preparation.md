#reactions/preparation
1. **By Dehydration Of Alcohol:**
	- $\ce{R-CH2-OH ->[H2SO4][413K] RC-O-CR}$
	- $\ce{R-CH2-OH ->[H2SO4][443K]R=CH2}$
		> $R = 1\degree ol$
		> $S_n2$
	
2. **Williamson:**
	- $\ce{RX + (R'/Ar)-O-Na+ -> R-O-(R'/Ar) + NaX}$
	> $S_n2$
	Secondary/tertiary alkyl halides lead to elimination (alkene formation) because alkoxides are strong bases.
