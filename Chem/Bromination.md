#### Amines:
- With bromine water: Forms 2,4,6-tribromoaniline (white precipitate) due to high reactivity.
- **Control Reactivity:** Protect -NH₂ group by acetylation with acetic anhydride (forms acetanilide). Then carry out substitution, followed by hydrolysis.
- **Acetanilide:** Lone pair on N is less available for donation to benzene ring (resonance with carbonyl oxygen), reducing activating effect.
```smiles
c1ccccc1N.[Br][Br]>>Nc1c(Br)cc(Br)cc1Br
```
#### Carboxylic Acid:
```smiles
c1ccccc1C(=O)O.[Br][Br]>>c1ccc(Br)cc1C(=O)O
```
