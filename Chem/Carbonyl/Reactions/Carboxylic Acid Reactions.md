#reactions 
### OH Removal:
	$\ce{R-COOH + M -> R-COOM + H2}$
	$\ce{R-COOH + MOH -> R-COOM + H2O}$
- **Acidity:** Carboxylic Acid > Phenols
	> $\ce{RCOO^- >_{Stable} PhO^-}$ ($e^-$ Delocalizes over 2 O)
- ### Substituency:
	- **EWG:** $\uparrow -COO^-$ Stability = $\uparrow$ Acidity
		- Ph < I < Br < Cl < F < CN < NO$_2$ < CF$_3$
	- **EDG:** $\downarrow -COO^-$ Stability = $\downarrow$ Acidity
	- **Attachment of Phenyl/Vinyl:** $\uparrow$ Acidity (> $sp_2$ $e^-$ withdrawing)
### COH Removal:
- **Anhydride Formation:**
	$\ce{RCOOH ->[H2SO4][P_2O5 + \Delta] R-CO-O-CO-R}$
- [[Esterification]]
- **PCl$_5$, PCl$_3$, SOCl$_2$:**
	$\ce{RCOOH ->[PCl_5, PCl_3, SOCl_2] RCOCl}$
	> SOCl$_2$ is prefered as byproducts (SO$_2$, HCl) are gaseous
- **Ammonia:**
	$\ce{RCOOH + NH3 -> RCOO^-NH4+ ->[\Delta] RCONH2}$
