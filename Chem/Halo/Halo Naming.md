### Nomenclature
- **Alkyl Halides:**
    - **Common:** Alkyl group + halide (e.g., n-propyl bromide).
        
    - **IUPAC:** Halosubstituted hydrocarbons (e.g., 1-bromopropane).
        
- **Aryl Halides:**
    - **Mono-halogenated:** Common and IUPAC names are the same (e.g., bromobenzene).
        
    - **Di-halogenated:** o-, m-, p- (common) or 1,2-, 1,3-, 1,4- (IUPAC).
        
- **Gem-dihalides:** Alkylidene halides (common).
- **Vic-dihalides:** Alkylene dihalides (common).
- **IUPAC for dihaloalkanes:** Dihaloalkanes.
