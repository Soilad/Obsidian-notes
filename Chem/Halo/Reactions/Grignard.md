$\ce{RX ->[Mg][dry ether] RMgX}$
- C-Mg: Highly polar covalent
- Mg-X: Ionic
#### Reaction with water/alcohol/amine
- $\ce{RMgX + H2O -> RH + Mg(OH)X}$
#### Reaction with aldehyde/ketone
```smiles
[Ra]C(=O)[Rb].[R][Mg][X]>>[Ra]C([R])([O-][Mg+][X])[Rb]
[Ra]C([R])([O-][Mg+][X])[Rb].[H][O+]([H])[H]>>[Ra]C(O)([R])[Rb].[Mg][Cl]O
```

```smiles
[R][Mg][X].O=C=O>>[R]C(=O)[O-]
```
