## Reflection:
- $\angle i = \angle r$
- $f = \frac{R}{2}$
- #### Mirror Equation:
	$\displaystyle \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u}$
- #### Magnification: $\displaystyle m = \frac{h'}{h} = \frac{-v}{u}$
## Refraction:
- #### Snell's Law:
	$\displaystyle n_21 = \frac{sin(i)}{sin(r)}$
- #### Refractive Index:
	- how much light bends when entering the medium
## Total Internal Reflection:
- Happens when angle of incidence exceeds $i_c$
- $\displaystyle sin(i_c) = \frac{n_2}{n1}$
- used for optical fibre, mirages, proving diamonds
## Lens Maker's Formula:
	$\displaystyle \frac{1}{f} = (n-1)(\frac{1}{R_1}-\frac{1}{R_2})$
- For Thin lens:
	$\displaystyle \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v}-\frac{1}{u}$
- Total Focus Lens:
	$\displaystyle\frac{1}{f} = \sum \frac{1}{f_i}$
