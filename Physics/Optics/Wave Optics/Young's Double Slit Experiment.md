## Approximations
- $D >> d \implies$ Both rays are considered as parallel
- $d >> \lambda$ 
- $\theta << \implies \sin\theta = \tan\theta \approx \theta = \frac \lambda d$
- $\therefore path\ difference, \Delta z = \frac \lambda d$
## Positions of Bright Fringes
$$
\begin{align}
\Delta z = n\lambda\\
x \frac dD = n\lambda\\
\implies x  = n\lambda\frac Dd\\
\end{align}
$$
## Positions of Dark Fringes
$$
\begin{align}
\Delta z = (2n+1)\frac\lambda 2\\
x \frac dD = (2n+1)\frac\lambda 2\\
\implies x  = (2n+1)\frac\lambda 2\frac Dd\\
\end{align}
$$
