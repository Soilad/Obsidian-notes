> Phenomenon of changing the path of light without any change in the medium
#### Laws of Reflection
1. $\angle i = \angle r$
2. $\angle i ,\angle r, N$ are co-planar
- #### Number of Reflections
	- $\displaystyle n = \frac{360}\theta - 1; \frac{360}\theta\ is\ even$
	- $\displaystyle n = \frac{360}\theta; \frac{360}\theta\ is\ odd$
- #### Total Internal Reflection:
	- Happens when angle of incidence exceeds $i_c$
	- From [[Snell's Law]] $\displaystyle \frac 1{sin(i_c)} = \frac{n_2}{n_1}$
	- used for optical fibre, mirages, proving diamonds
