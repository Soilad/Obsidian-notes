- #### Lens Maker's Formula:
	$\displaystyle \frac{1}{f} = (n-1)(\frac{1}{R_1}-\frac{1}{R_2})$
	- $\mu$: IOR of lens w.r.t surrounding medium
	- $R_1,R_2$: Radii of curvature for both surfaces
	- #### For Thin lens:
		- $\displaystyle \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{v}-\frac{1}{u}$
		- [[Convex Lens]]
		- [[Concave Lens]]
	- #### Total Focus Lens:
		- $\displaystyle\frac{1}{f} = \sum \frac{1}{f_i}$
