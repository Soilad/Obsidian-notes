## For [[Concave Mirror]] and [[Convex Lens]]

| Object Position                   | Image Position                        | Image Size                      | Nature of Image     |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| At infinity                       | At focus (F₂)                         | Highly diminished (point-sized) | Real and inverted   |
| Beyond 2F₁                        | Between F₂ and 2F₂                    | Diminished                      | Real and inverted   |
| At 2F₁                            | At 2F₂                                | Same size as object             | Real and inverted   |
| Between F₁ and 2F₁                | Beyond 2F₂                            | Magnified (enlarged)            | Real and inverted   |
| At focus (F₁)                     | At infinity                           | Highly magnified                | Real and inverted   |
| Between F₁ and optical centre (O) | On the same side as object, beyond F₁ | Magnified                       | Virtual and upright |
