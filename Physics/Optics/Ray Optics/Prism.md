## At Angle of Minimum Deviation ($\delta_m$)
- $i_1 = i_2$
- $r_1=r_2$
- $\delta = \delta_{m}$
$$
\begin{align}
2(\frac \pi2 -r)+A = \pi\\
\pi -2r+A = \pi\\
A = 2r\\
2(i-r)+(\pi - \delta_m) = \pi\\
2i-2r - \delta_m = 0\\
2i = A + \delta_m\\
\mu = \frac{\sin(i)}{\sin(r)} = \frac{\sin(\frac{A+\delta_m}{2})}{\sin(\frac{A}{2})}\\
\implies \mu \approx \frac{A+\delta_m}{A} \approx 1+\frac{\delta_m}{A}\\
\implies \delta_m = (\mu-1)A\\
\end{align}
$$
## Angular Dispersion
> is the difference in $\delta$ for 2 extreme colours 
- Violet: $\delta_V = (\mu_V-1)A$
- Red: $\delta_R = (\mu_R-1)A$
- Yellow ($\bar \delta$): $\delta_V = (\mu_V-1)A$
- #### Dispersive Power $(\omega)$:
	- $\omega = \frac{\mu_V-\mu_R}{\mu_Y} = \frac{\delta_V-\delta_R}\delta$
	- $(\mu_V-\mu_R)A = \delta_V-\delta_R$
