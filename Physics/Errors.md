#### True Value:
$\bar{x}$
#### Absolute Error:
$\Delta{x_i} = (x_i-\bar{x})$
#### Mean Absolute Error:
$\bar{(\Delta{x})} = \frac{\sum{\Delta{x_i}}}{n}$
#### Relative/Fractional Error:
$\frac{\bar{\Delta{x}}}{\bar{x}}$
#### Percentage Error:
$\frac{\bar{\Delta{x}}}{\bar{x}}100$
#### Addition of Error:
$x_i = \bar{x}\pm\Delta{x_i}$
$=> \sum{x_i} = \sum{\bar{x}} \pm \sum{\Delta{x_i}}$
##### Example:
$a = 5 \pm0.3$
$b = 3 \pm0.5$
$=> a+b = 8 \pm0.8$
$=> a-b = 2 \pm0.8$
error always gets added in addition/subtraction
#### Multiplication of Error:
$Q = a^nb^m$
$=> \frac{\Delta{Q}}{Q} = |n|\frac{\Delta{a}}{a}+|m|\frac{\Delta{b}}{b}$
> if ur trying to find the error of an unknown in a equation, rearrange the equation to separate out the unknown and the find the error
