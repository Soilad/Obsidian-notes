#reactions/preparation 
1. **Alkenes:**
	- (Markonikov's rule)
	```smiles
	c=cc(c)c>O.[H+]>cc(O)c(c)c
	```
	 - **Hydroboration-Oxidation:** (Anti-Markonikov)
	```smiles
	c=cc(c)c.[H]1[BH2][H][BH2]1>OO>Occc(c)c
	```
2. **Carbonyl Compounds:**
	- Aldehyde/Ketone Reduction:
		$\ce{RCHO ->[H2][Pd/Pt/Ni/NaBH4/LiAlH4] RCH2OH}$
		$\ce{RCOR' ->[H2][Pd/Pt/Ni/NaBH4/LiAlH4] RCH(OH)R'}$
	- Oxalic acid/Ester Reduction:
		$\ce{RCOOH ->[LiAlH4][H2O] RCH2OH}$
		$\ce{RCOOR' ->[LiAlH4][H2O] RCH2OH + HOR'}$
		
3. **[[Grignard Reagent]]:**
	$\ce{R1-CO-R2 ->[R3MgX] R1R2R3COH}$
