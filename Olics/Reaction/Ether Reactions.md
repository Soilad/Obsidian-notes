#reactions 
1. **CO Bond Cleavage:**
	>Mechanism: Protonation of ether → nucleophilic attack by halide ion (Sₙ2 or Sₙ1 depending on alkyl group). 
	
	- Di-alkyl:
		- $\ce{ROR + HX -> R-X + R-OH}$
	- Alkyl Aryl:
		- $\ce{ArOR + HX -> R-X + Ar-OH}$
	- Mixed Ethers:
		- $\ce{ROR' + HX -> R-X + R'-OH}$
		> $S_n2: (R\land R' \in [1\degree,2\degree]) \land (n(R)<n(R'))$
		> $S_n1: (R = 3\degree)$
		
	- **Anisole:**
```smiles
c1ccccc1OC>>c1ccccc1O.CI
```
2. **Electrophilic Substitution:** (Ar-O-R)
>  **Alkoxy Group (-OR):** Ortho-, para-directing and activates the aromatic ring (due [[Aryl Resonance]]).

- **Halogenation:**
	$\ce{Ar-O-R + X2 -> p-X-Ar-O-R (Major)}$
	
- **[[Friedel-Craft]]:**	
```smiles
c1ccccc1OC.[Al](Cl)(Cl)(Cl).[R][X]>>[R]c1ccccc1OC.c1cc([R])ccc1OC
```
```smiles
c1ccccc1OC.[Al](Cl)(Cl)(Cl).[Ar][X]>>[Ar]c1ccccc1OC.c1cc([Ar])ccc1OC
```
- **[[Nitration]]:**
```smiles
c1ccccc1OC.S(=O)(=O)(O)(O).N(=O)O>>N(=O)(O)c1ccccc1OC.c1cc(N(=O)O)ccc1OC
```
