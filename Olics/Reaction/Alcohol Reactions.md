### Alcohols
##### Cleavage of OH:
1. **Metals:**
	- $\ce{(R/Ph)-OH + M -> (R/Ph)-OM + H2}; M\in[Na, K, Al]$
	- $\ce{Ph-OH + Aq NaOH -> NaO-Ph}$
2. **Esterification:**[[Carboxylic Acid Reactions]]
	- $\ce{(R/Ph)-OH + R'COO(H/Cl/OR') <=>[Conc H2SO4] R'COO(R/Ph) + H+(OH-/Cl-/R')}$
	- **Aspirin Synthesis:**
	```smiles
	C(=O)(O)c1ccccc1O.Cc(=O)oc(=O)C>>O(C(=O)C)c1ccccc1C(=O)(O).Cc(=O)(O)
	```
##### Cleavage of CO:
1. **Hydrogen Halides**:
	$\ce{ROH + HX -> RX + H2O}$
	- Lucas Test:
		$\ce{ROH ->[HCl][Anhy ZnCl2] RCCl + H2O}$

| Degree     | Observation          |
| :--------- | :------------------- |
| 1$\degree$ | no reaction          |
| 2$\degree$ | slow reaction        |
| 3$\degree$ | really fast reaction |
2. **Phosphorous Trihalides:**
	$\ce{R-OH ->[PX3] R-X}; X \in [Cl, Br, I]$
3. **Dehydration:**
	$\ce{R-C-OH ->[Conc H2SO4/H3PO4][Anhy ZnCl/ Al2O3, 443K] R=C}$
	- 3$\degree$>2$\degree$>1$\degree$(carbocation stability)
	
##### Dehydrogenation:
1. **Oxidizing:** [[Carbonyl Preparation]]
	- Primary:
		$\ce{RCH2OH ->[Anhy CrO3][PCC] RCHO}$
		$\ce{RCH2OH ->[H+ KMnO4] RCHO}$
	- Secondary:
		$\ce{RCH(OH)R' ->[Anhy CrO3][PCC] R(CO)R'}$
		
2. **Catalytic Dehydrogenation:** (Cu at 573K)
