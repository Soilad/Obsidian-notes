#reactions 
1. **Sandmeyer:**
	$\ce{Ar-N2^{+}X^{-} ->[CuCl/HCl or CuBr/HBr or CuCN/KCN] Ar-(Cl or Br or CN) + N2}$
2. **Gattermann:**
	$\ce{Ar-N2+X^-->[KI]ArI}$
	> no $CuI$ required
3. **Replace Fluorine:** 
	$\ce{Ar-N2+Cl- HBF4->Ar-N2+(BF4)->[\Delta]Ar-F + BF3 +N2}$
4. **H Replacement:**
	$\ce{Ar-N2+X- ->[H3PO4] ArH}$
5. **OH Replacement:**
	$\ce{Ar-N2+X- ->[H2O][\Delta] ArOH}$
6. **NO2 Replacement:
	$\ce{Ar-N2+(BF4)- + NaNO2 + Cu ->[\Delta] ArNO2}$
7. **Coupling Reactions: ($N2+$ stays) [[Azo Dye]]

```smiles
c1ccccc1[N+]#N.Oc1ccccc1>>OC1=CC=C(C=C1)N=NC2=CC=CC=C2
```
