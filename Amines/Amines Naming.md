- **Amines:**
    - **Primary amines**: "Alkanamine" (replace 'e' of alkane with 'amine').
	
    - **Multiple amino groups:** Specify positions with numbers and prefixes (e.g., ethane-1,2-diamine).
	
    - **Secondary/Tertiary:** Use locant 'N' to designate substituents on nitrogen (e.g., N-methylethanamine, N,N-diethylethanamine).
	
    - **Arylamines:** -NH₂ directly attached to benzene ring. Aniline is an accepted IUPAC name. Suffix 'e' of arene replaced by 'amine' (e.g., benzenamine).

- Diazonuim Salt:
	- Suffix "diazonium" to parent hydrocarbon name, followed by anion name (e.g., benzenediazonium chloride).
