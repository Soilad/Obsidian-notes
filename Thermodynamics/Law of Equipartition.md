> $f$: Degree of freedom
## For each degree
$$
\begin{align}
positional:
\frac{mv_x^2}2, \frac{mv_y^2}2, \frac{mv_z^2}2;(f = 3)\\
rotational:
\frac{mv_{xy}^2}2, \frac{mv_{yz}^2}2, \frac{mv_{zx}^2}2;(f+3)\\
\implies \sum^f \frac{mv_i^2}2 = \sum^f \frac 12K_bT\\
\end{align}
$$
