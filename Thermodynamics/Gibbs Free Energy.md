## $\displaystyle \Delta G = \Delta H - T\Delta S$
- [[State Function]]
- Determines Reaction's [[Spontaneity]]
### At Constant Temperature & Pressure
|$\Delta G$|[[Spontaneity]]|
|--|--|
|$\Delta G < 0$| Spontaneous|
|$\Delta G > 0$| Non-spontaneous|
|$\Delta G = 0$| [[Equilibrium]]|
#### On Temperature change
| $\Delta H$     | $\Delta S$     | [[Spontaneity]]                                      |     |
| -------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | --- |
| $\Delta H < 0$ | $\Delta S > 0$ | Always spontaneous                                   |     |
| $\Delta H > 0$ | $\Delta S > 0$ | Spontaneous at high Temperature ([[Entropy]] driven) |     |
| $\Delta H < 0$ | $\Delta S < 0$ | Spontaneous at Low Temperature ([[Enthalpy]] driven) |     |
| $\Delta H > 0$ | $\Delta S < 0$ | Always Non-spontaneous                               |     |
