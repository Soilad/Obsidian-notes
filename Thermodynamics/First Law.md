> Energy cant be created or destroyed but it can be converted from one form to another

## In Physics
#### $\displaystyle \Delta U = Q -W$
## In Chemistry
#### $\displaystyle \Delta U = \Delta H +W$
- $U$: Internal Energy
- $Q$: [[Heat]]
- $H$: [[Enthalpy]]
- $W$: [[Work]]
#### Sign Convention:
- **(+)**: Energy is given to the system
- **(-)**: Energy is taken from the system
