- [[State Function]]
- $S_{gas}>S_{liquid}>S_{solid}$
- $\Delta S_{total}=0 \implies$ Reversible/[[Equilibrium]]
#### Increases when:
- Phase transition from solid → liquid → gas.
- Temperature increases.
- Number of moles of gas increases in a reaction.
- A substance dissolves.
####  At constant T&P
- $\displaystyle \Delta S_{phase\ transition} = \frac{\Delta H_{trans}}{T_trans}$
- $\displaystyle \Delta S_{surrounding} = -\frac{Q_{sys}}{T_{sys}} = -\frac{\Delta H_{sys}}{T_{sys}}$
