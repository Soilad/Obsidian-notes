#### $\Delta_r G\degree = - RT\ln(K)$
| $\Delta_r G\degree$ | $K$   | Favours |
| ---------------- | --- | ------- |
| $\Delta_r G\degree<0$ | $K>1$   | Products favour [[Equilibrium]] |
| $\Delta_r G\degree>0$ | $K<1$   | Reactants favour [[Equilibrium]] |
| $\Delta_r G\degree=0$ | $K=1$   |  |
- [[Gibbs Free Energy]] change for reactions where all reactants and products are in standard state
- $\displaystyle \Delta_r G \degree = \sum \nu_p \Delta_f G \degree_{(products)} - \sum \nu_p \Delta_f G \degree_{(reactants)}$
