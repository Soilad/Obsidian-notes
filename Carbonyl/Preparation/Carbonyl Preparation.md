#reactions/preparation 
## Diisobutylaluminum hydride (DIBAL-H)
```smiles
[Al](CC(C)(C))(CC(C)(C))([H]1)[H][Al]1(CC(C)(C))(CC(C)(C))
```
### General Methods:
1. **From Alcohol:** [[Alcohol Reactions]]
	- **Oxidation**
	- Dehydrogenation
2. **From Hydrocarbons:**
- **Ozonolysis of Alkenes:**
	$\ce{R-C=C-R' + O3 + ->[Zn][H2O] RCHO + R'CHO}$
- **Hydration of Alkynes:**
	$\ce{C\equiv CR ->[H2SO4] RCOCH3}$
### Specific Methods For Aldehydes:
1. **Acyl Chloride:** (Rosenmund Reduction)
	- $\ce{ROCl ->[H2/Pd-BaSO4] ROH}$
2. **Nitriles and Esters:**
	- Stephen:
		$\ce{RCN ->[SnCl2/HCl] R=CNH ->[H2O] RCHO}$
	- DIBAL-H:
		$\ce{RCN or RCOOR' ->[DIBAL-H][H2O] RCHO}$
3. **Hydrocarbons: (Aromatic Aldehydes)**
	1. **Toluene Oxidation**
		- **Etard reaction:** $\ce{C6H5CH3 ->[CrO2Cl2][CS2] C6H5CHO}$
		```smiles
		c1ccccc1C[H].O=[Cr](=O)([Cl])[Cl].C(=S)=S>>C=C1C=CC=CC1[Cr](=O)(Cl)(Cl)O
		C=C1C=CC=CC1[Cr](=O)(Cl)(Cl)O>>c1ccccc1C([H])O[Cr](Cl)(Cl)O.[Cl-]
		c1ccccc1C([H])O[Cr](Cl)(Cl)O.[Cl-]>>c1ccccc1C([H])=O.[Cr](Cl)O
		```
		- **Chromic Oxide:** $\ce{C6H5CH3 ->[CrO3][Acetic Anhy] C6H5CHO}$
		```smiles
		c1ccccc1C.[Cr]([O-])([O-])=O.CC(=O)OC(=O)C>>c1ccccc1C(OC(=O)C)(OC(=O)C)
		c1ccccc1C(OC(=O)C)(OC(=O)C)>[O][H][H][H]>c1ccccc1C(=O)[H]
		```
	2. **Side Chain Chlorination + Hydrolysis:**
		$\ce{C6H5CH3 ->[Cl2][hv] C6H5CH2Cl ->[H2O] C6H5CH2OH}$
	3. **Gatterman-Koch:**
		$\ce{C6H6 / C6H6 derivative ->[CO/HCl][AlCl3/CuCl] C6H5CHO}$
### Specific Methods For Ketones:
> [[Grignard Reagent]]
1. **Acyl Chlorides:**
	- $\ce{2RMgX + CdCl2 -> R2Cd + 2MgXCl}$ 
	- $\ce{RCOCl ->[R'2Cd] RCOR'}$
2. **Nitriles:**
	- $\ce{RCN + R'MgX ->[H2O] RCOR'}$
3. **Aryl:**
	- **[[Friedel-Craft]] Acylation:**
	$\ce{Ar + R-CO-Cl ->[AlCl3] R-CO-Ar}$
