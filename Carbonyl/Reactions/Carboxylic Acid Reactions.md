### OH Removal:
	$\ce{R-COOH + M -> R-COOM + H2}$
	$\ce{R-COOH + MOH -> R-COOM + H2O}$
- **Acidity:** Carboxylic Acid > Phenols
	> $\ce{RCOO^- >_{Stable} PhO^-}$ ($e^-$ Delocalizes over 2 O)
- ### Substituency:
	- **EWG:** $\uparrow -COO^-$ Stability = $\uparrow$ Acidity
		- Ph < I < Br < Cl < F < CN < NO$_2$ < CF$_3$
	- **EDG:** $\downarrow -COO^-$ Stability = $\downarrow$ Acidity
	- **Attachment of Phenyl/Vinyl:** $\uparrow$ Acidity (> $sp_2$ $e^-$ withdrawing)
### COH Removal:
- **Anhydride Formation:**
	$\ce{RCOOH ->[H2SO4][P_2O5 + \Delta] R-CO-O-CO-R}$
- **Esterification:** [[Alcohol Reactions]]
	$\ce{RCOOH + R'OH ->[Conc H2SO4][HCl_{(g)}] RCOOR'}$
- **PCl$_5$, PCl$_3$, SOCl$_2$:**
	$\ce{RCOOH ->[PCl_5, PCl_3, SOCl_2] RCOCl}$
	> SOCl$_2$ is prefered as byproducts (SO$_2$, HCl) are gaseous
- **Ammonia:**
	$\ce{RCOOH + NH3 -> RCOO^-NH4+ ->[\Delta] RCONH2}$
### COOH Involving:
1. **Reduction:**
	$\ce{RCOOH ->[LiAlH4, B2H6][\cancel{NaBH4}] RCH2OH}$
2. **Decarboxylation:** (-1 C)
	$\ce{RCOONa + 3NaOH + CaO-> RH + Na2CO3}$
		- Kolbe Electrolysis:
	$\ce{RCOONa + 2H2O->[Electrolysis] R-R + 2CO2 + 2H2 2NaOH}$
### Substitution Reaction
1. **Hell-Volhard-Zelinsky**
	$\ce{RCH3COOH ->[Br2][Red P, H2O] RCH2BrCOOH}$
2. **Ring Substitution:**
	- [[Nitration]] -> m-nitrobenzoic acid
	- [[Bromination]] -> m-bromobenzoic acid
	- [[Friedel-Craft]] $\not->$ (C=O Group Deactivates & Complexes AlCl$_3$)
