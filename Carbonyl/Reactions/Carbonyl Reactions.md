## Sodium Potassium Tartarate (Rochelle's Salt)
```smiles
[O-]C(=O)C(O)C(O)C(=O)[O-].[Na+].[K+]
```
1. **Nucleophilic Addition:**
> Nucleophile attacks C in C=O, Tetrahedral alkoxide is formed, then protonated
> **Reactivity:** Aldehydes > Ketones
	[[Steric Hindrance]]
	[[+I Effect]]
	[[Aryl Resonance]]
- **HCN Addition:**
	- $\ce{R-CO-R' + HCN ->[OH-] RR'C(OH)CN}$
- **Sodium Bisulphite:**
```smiles
[R]C=O[R].O=S(O[Na])(O)>>C(OS(=O)O)([O-][Na+])([R])[R]
C(OS(=O)O)([O-][Na+])([R])[R]>>C(OS(=O)O[Na])(O)([R])[R]
```
- **[[Grignard Reagent]] Addition:**
- **Alcohol Addition:**
	$\ce{RCHO <=>[R'OH][HCl(g)] R-(CH2OH)-OR' _{(Hemiacetal)}<=>[R'OH][H+] R-COH-(OR')2 _{(Acetal)}}$
	> 	Alkoxyalcohol intermediate
	> 	gem-Dialkoxy
	```smiles
	[R]C(=O)[R].OCCO>[Cl]>[R]C1([R])OCCO1.O
	[R]C1([R])OCCO1.O>[Cl]>[R]C(=O)[R].OCCO
	```
- **Ammonia:** [[Azo Dye]]
```smiles
[R]C(=O)[R].[H+]>>[R][C+](O)[R]
[R][C+](O)[R].N[Z]>>C(O)([R])([R])[N+]([H])([H])[Z]
C(O)([R])([R])[N+]([H])([H])[Z]>>OC([R])([R])N[Z].[H+]
OC([R])([R])N[Z]>>[Z]N=C([R])([R]).O
```
2. **Reduction:**
	- **To Alcohols:**
		$\ce{R-CO-R' ->[NaBH4/LiAlH4][H2, Pd/C] R-CH2OH-R'}$
	- **To Hydrocarbons:**
		- Clemmensen:
			$\ce{R-CO-R' ->[Zn-Hg][Conc HCl] R-CH2-R'}$
		- Wolf-Kishner:
			$\ce{R-CO-R' ->[H2NNH2][KOH/HOCH3CH3OH _{(ethylene-glycol)}] R-CH2-R'}$
3. **Oxidation:**
	> Aldehydes easily oxidise; ketones require vigorous conditions
	- **Aldehydes**
		$\ce{RCHO ->[HNO3/KMnO4/K2CrO7] RCOOH}$
		- **Mild Oxidizing Agents:
			- **Tollens' Test:**
			(Tollens' reagent) -> Ammonical Silver Nitrate
			$\ce{RCHO + 2[Ag(NH3)]+OH- ->[\Delta] RCOONH4 + \downarrow2Ag +3NH3 + H2O}$
			- **Fehling's Test: $(R \not= Ar)$
			$\ce{RCHO ->[Aq CuSO4][KNaC4H4O6 (Rochelle's Salt)] RCOOH + \downarrow Cu2O + 3H2O}$
	- **Ketones**
		- **Haloform Test:** $\ce{X \in [Cl, Br, I]}$
			Iodoform test: 
				Detects ($\ce{CH3CO -}$) or ($\ce{CH3CHOH - ->CH3CO -}$)
			$\ce{R-CO-CH3 ->[OH-][X2] R-CO-CX3 ->[OH-] R-CO^-OH-CX3 -> R-COOH + ^-CX3 -> R-COO- + HCX3}$
4. **Aldol Condensation:** 
![](Carbonyl/Diagrams/Enolate_aldol_mechanism.png)
	- **Enol -> Aldol Addition Product:
```smiles
[Ra]C(=O)C.[H+]>>[Ra]C(O)=C
[Ra]C(O)=C.[Rb]C=O>>[Ra]C(=O)CC([Rb])O
```
- **Enolate -> Aldol Addition Product:
```smiles
[Ra]C(=O)C.[M+][OH]>>[Ra]C([O-][M+])=C
[Ra]C([O-][M+])=C.[Rb]C=O>>[Ra]C(=O)CC([Rb])([O-][M+])
[Ra]C(=O)CC([Rb])([O-][M+])>>[Ra]C(=O)CC([Rb])O
```
- **Condensation:
```smiles
[Ra]C(=O)CC([Rb])O>>[Ra]C(=O)C=C[Rb].O
```
5. **Canizzaro:**
	> Self-oxidation and reduction (disproportionation).
	- $\ce{RCHO ->[OH-] RCOO- + RCH2OH}$
